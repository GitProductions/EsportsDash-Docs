## DuoCam Row

**DuoCam Row** is a dual camera setup for player and commentator views. 
<Image src="/images/overlays/default/duocam-row.webp" alt="DuoCam Row" />


## TrioCam Row

**TrioCam Row** is a three-camera setup: two for shoutcasters, one for player or host. 
<Image src="/images/overlays/default/triocam-row.webp" alt="TrioCam Row"/>

## Analyst Special
**Analyst Special** is a two-camera setup for your shoutcasters. 
<Image src="/images/overlays/default/analystspecial.webp" alt="Analyst Special"/>


## Duo Single Cam
**Duo Single Cam** is a full screen single camera setup for multiple commentators. 
<Image src="/images/overlays/default/duo-singlecam.webp" alt="Duo Single Cam"/>

## Map Pool
**Map Pool** is a display of current maps & scores for the match.
<Image src="/images/overlays/default/map-pool.webp" alt="Map Pool" />

## Team
**Team** displays each member of the team and their selected roles & characters. 
<Image src="/images/overlays/default/teams.webp" alt="Team Scene" />

## Bracket
**Bracket** is a Single Elimination & Double Elimination Bracket overview.
<Image src="/images/overlays/default/bracket.webp" alt="Bracket" />

## Away Screen
**Away Screen** is an intermission screen which showcases matches upcoming. 
<Image src="/images/overlays/default/away.webp" alt="Away Screen" />
